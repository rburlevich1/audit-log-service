# Spec Evaluation Checklist

- Each acceptance criterion is testable.
- Pagination strategy is justified.
- Tasks have references and a clear DoD.
- Dependencies between tasks are explicit.
- Open questions are explicit and not silently answered in design or plans.
- Design choices align with `AGENTS.md` invariants.
- List endpoints define deterministic sorting with a tiebreaker.
- Verification includes the relevant unit or integration tests.
